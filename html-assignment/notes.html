HTML Tag Dictionary

Home Notes
<strong>
Denotes strong emphasis on text, typically displayed in bold.
<blockquote>
Specifies a section that is quoted from another source, often used for citations.
<footer>
Defines the footer for a document or a section, typically containing authorship information, copyright notices, and other important links.
<em>
Emphasizes text, usually italicizing it, to convey a different tone or level of importance.
<nav>
Defines a set of navigation links, helping with the organization of and navigation to different sections of a site or page.
